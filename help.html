Our Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I calculate my train fare?

Select your departure and destination stations on the fare calculator page, and the system will compute the fare automatically.

2. What are the operating hours of the train?

Trains operate from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Check the schedule page for more details.

3. Are there discounts for students and senior citizens?

Yes, discounted fares are available for students, senior citizens, and PWDs. Make sure to bring valid identification to use the discount.

4. Where can I buy tickets?

Tickets can be purchased at any station ticket booth or through our online ticketing system.
